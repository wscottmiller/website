About Me

I am an aspiring software engineer and current third-year computer science student at the University of California San Diego. I am also a veteran of the U.S. Navy, where I worked as an Aegis computer display technician and developed my passion for computers. Upon my honorable discharge from the service I decided to pursue my B.S. in computer science. I earned my Associates in Science and Mathematics at Grossmont and Cuyamaca Community Colleges, and transferred to my dream school, UCSD in La Jolla, CA in 2014. I am attracted to all things related to computer software and technology. I am passionate about traveling, and the great outdoors. You can find me on Facebook and Linkedin and see what I'm up to.
